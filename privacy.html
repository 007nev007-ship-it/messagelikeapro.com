PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy matters.

This policy explains how Message Like A Pro handles basic website, product, application, and enquiry-related information.

The website currently provides information about the MLAP communication support system, products, research foundation, and future developments.

Last updated: June 2026

This policy may be updated as the website, applications, products, and communication support ecosystem continue to evolve.

Respectful handling of information.

Message Like A Pro respects user privacy and aims to handle enquiry-related information responsibly.


Personal information is not sold.

Privacy Overview

The following principles explain how information may be collected, used, and handled.

Information You Provide

If you contact MLAP by email, you may provide your name, email address, organisation, enquiry type, and message content.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to respond to enquiries, discuss product access, provide support, or share relevant information about MLAP.

Information Sharing

Personal information is not sold. Information is only shared where necessary to operate services or comply with legal requirements.

Website Data

Basic technical information may be processed by hosting services, browsers, security systems, or analytics services depending upon website configuration.

Third-Party Services

The website may use third-party providers such as hosting, email services, payment services, application platforms, or analytics providers.

Data Security

Reasonable care is taken to protect information, although no website, email system, or digital platform can guarantee complete security.

Your Choices

Users may contact MLAP regarding personal information provided through direct communication.

Correction

You may request correction of personal information that you have provided through direct communication.

Deletion

You may request deletion of personal information where reasonably possible and where retention is not legally required.

Children

The website is intended for professional, educational, and organisational audiences and is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Future Development

As the MLAP ecosystem develops, additional privacy provisions may be introduced where required.

Applications

Future applications may contain their own privacy information depending upon functionality and platform requirements.

Products

Product purchases, digital access, or future services may require additional privacy information.

Policy Updates

Privacy policies may be updated periodically as the communication support ecosystem continues to develop.

Questions about privacy?

For privacy-related enquiries, please contact Message Like A Pro using the information provided on the contact page.
