Privacy Policy

Privacy matters.

This page explains how Message Like A Pro handles basic website and enquiry-related information.

Last Updated

June 2026

This policy may be updated as the website, app, products, and communication support ecosystem continue to develop.

Overview

Message Like A Pro respects user privacy.

Message Like A Pro is a research-informed communication support system. This website is currently used to provide information about the system, the book, the app, the research foundation, and future development.

Information You Provide

If you contact Message Like A Pro by email, the information you provide may include your name, email address, organisation, and message content.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to respond to enquiries, manage communication, discuss potential projects, and provide relevant information about MLAP.

Information Sharing

Personal information is not sold. Information is only shared where necessary to respond to enquiries, operate services, or comply with legal requirements.

Website Data

Basic technical data may be processed by hosting services, browsers, analytics tools, or security systems depending on how the website is hosted and maintained.

Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services such as hosting providers, email providers, analytics tools, or app distribution platforms.

Data Security

Reasonable care is taken to protect information, but no website, email system, or digital service can guarantee complete security.

Your Choices

You may contact Message Like A Pro to request correction or deletion of personal information provided through direct communication, where reasonably possible.

Children

This website is intended for professional, educational, and organisational audiences. It is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Communication support should be responsible, respectful, and professionally appropriate.
