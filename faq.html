About Message Like A Pro

Understanding the communication support system.

What is Message Like A Pro?

Message Like A Pro is a research-informed communication support system designed to improve communication outcomes within multilingual workplaces.

Is MLAP a book?

MLAP includes six books, but it is larger than a single publication. The wider system includes apps, templates, communication methods, and workplace support resources.

Is MLAP an app?

Two apps support the wider ecosystem. The MLAP App focuses on professional communication support while BridgeEDU supports educational practice.

Research and Development

Is MLAP research-informed?

Yes. MLAP was developed in response to communication research exploring communication challenges within multilingual educational workplaces.

Is the system scientifically proven?

No. MLAP does not claim to be scientifically proven, experimentally validated, or clinically verified.

What problem does MLAP address?

Many workplaces provide communication technology but offer little communication support. MLAP was developed to help address that support gap.

The Communication Support Gap

What is the support gap?

The support gap exists when organisations provide communication technology but do not provide communication guidance, frameworks, examples, or practical support.

Does MLAP replace technology?

No. MLAP complements communication technology by supporting communication decisions, wording, tone, and outcomes.

Why is this important?

Communication problems may continue even when digital tools are available because communication effectiveness depends upon more than technology.

Books, Communication Support, and Apps

How many books are there?

The ecosystem currently contains six books covering communication, conflict, workplace pressure, leadership, and professional communication support.

Is MLAP mainly about templates?

No. Templates are one practical delivery mechanism, but the real value is the wider communication support system: judgement, tone, structure, professional decision-making, and clearer workplace outcomes.

Can templates be adapted?

Yes. Templates provide communication support and can be adjusted to suit individual situations, relationships, and workplace contexts.

Who Is MLAP For?

Who can use MLAP?

Teachers, educational professionals, international staff, multilingual workplace employees, and professionals working across cultures may find the system useful.

Is it only for teachers?

The current system focuses primarily on educational workplaces, although many communication situations extend to wider professional contexts.

Can organisations use the system?

Educational organisations and multilingual workplaces may find communication support frameworks useful for staff development and workplace communication.
