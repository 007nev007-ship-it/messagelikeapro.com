The MLAP System

A communication support system designed to improve outcomes.

Message Like A Pro operates within the communication support layer: the space between communication technology and communication outcomes.

System Position

The book is not the system. The app is not the system. The communication system is the product.

The book, app, templates, and website are delivery mechanisms for the wider MLAP communication support system.

Technology delivers messages. Communication systems improve outcomes.
The MLAP Principle

The Five-Layer Model

Communication effectiveness depends on more than technology.

MLAP explains communication through five connected layers: the communication environment, the technology used, the support available, the effectiveness of the communication, and the outcome it creates.

01 — Communication Environment

Multilingual communication, culture, hierarchy, power distance, uncertainty, workplace expectations, and professional relationships shape how communication is interpreted.

02 — Communication Technology

Email, LINE, messaging apps, translation tools, group chats, and digital systems deliver messages and improve access.

03 — Communication Support

Guidance, frameworks, templates, strategies, examples, and communication decision support help people communicate more effectively.

04 — Communication Effectiveness

Effective communication improves clarity, understanding, interpretation, confidence, judgement, and professional communication decisions.

05 — Communication Outcomes

Better communication can support improved relationships, reduced misunderstanding, reduced conflict, stronger coordination, trust, and workplace effectiveness.

Where MLAP Operates

MLAP does not replace communication technology. It supports the communication that technology delivers.

The system provides practical support for the communication decisions that happen before, during, and after a message is sent.

Before the message

MLAP helps users pause, clarify the situation, consider risk, identify the communication outcome, and choose an appropriate approach.

During the message

MLAP supports tone, structure, professional wording, cultural awareness, hierarchy sensitivity, and clear communication.

After the message

MLAP supports follow-up, documentation, clarification, relationship protection, and communication stability.
