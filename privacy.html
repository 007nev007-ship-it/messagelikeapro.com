PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy matters.

This policy explains how Message Like A Pro handles basic website, product, app, and enquiry-related information.

The website currently provides information about the MLAP communication support system, products, research foundation, and future development.

Last updated: June 2026

This policy may be updated as the website, applications, products, and communication support ecosystem continue to develop.

Respectful handling of information.

Message Like A Pro respects user privacy and aims to handle enquiry-related information responsibly.


Personal information is not sold.

Privacy Overview

The following principles explain how information may be collected, used, and handled.

Information You Provide

If you contact MLAP by email, you may provide your name, email address, organisation, enquiry type, and message content.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to respond to enquiries, manage communication, discuss product access, or provide relevant information about MLAP.

Information Sharing

Personal information is not sold. Information is only shared where necessary to respond to enquiries, operate services, or comply with legal requirements.

Website Data

Basic technical data may be processed by hosting services, browsers, analytics tools, or security systems depending on how the website is hosted and maintained.

Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services such as hosting providers, email providers, analytics tools, payment providers, or app distribution platforms.

Data Security

Reasonable care is taken to protect information, but no website, email system, or digital service can guarantee complete security.

Your Choices

Users may contact MLAP regarding personal information provided through direct communication.

Correction

You may request correction of personal information you have provided through direct communication.

Deletion

You may request deletion of personal information where reasonably possible and where retention is not required.

Children

This website is intended for professional, educational, and organisational audiences. It is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Questions about privacy?

For privacy-related enquiries, contact Message Like A Pro using the contact information provided on this website.
