MLAP PRODUCT ECOSYSTEM

One communication system. Practical products for real professional situations.

Message Like A Pro is delivered through a flagship book, a professional app, and connected communication resources that help teachers communicate with greater clarity, confidence, and judgement in multilingual educational workplaces.

The products are not separate random resources. They are practical access points into the same research-informed communication support system.

View The Flagship System View The App

The system is the product.

The book, app, and supporting resources all serve one purpose: better professional communication when workplace situations are unclear, sensitive, or high-pressure.

Products designed around communication outcomes

MLAP products support the professional thinking behind communication: choosing tone, understanding context, managing pressure, reducing misunderstanding, and responding with clarity.


The aim is not simply to give teachers something to copy. The aim is to help them make stronger communication decisions in real workplace situations.

What The Products Help With

MLAP supports users before, during, and after difficult communication moments.

Clarify the situation, consider the audience, identify the risk, and choose a professional approach.

While Writing

Use structured examples, professional wording, and communication strategies to create clearer messages.
