Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about the Message Like A Pro communication support system, books, apps, research, and workplace communication resources.

About Message Like A Pro

Understanding the communication support system.

What is Message Like A Pro?

Message Like A Pro is a research-informed communication support system designed to improve communication outcomes within multilingual educational workplaces.

Is MLAP a book?

MLAP includes six books, but it is larger than a single publication. The wider system includes apps, templates, communication methods, and workplace support resources.

Is MLAP an app?

Two apps support the wider ecosystem. The MLAP App focuses on professional communication support, while the BridgeEDU App supports classroom and educational practice.

Research and Development

Is MLAP research-based?

MLAP is research-informed and was developed through communication research exploring communication challenges within multilingual educational workplaces.

Is the system scientifically proven?

No. MLAP does not claim to be scientifically proven, experimentally validated, or clinically verified.

What problem does MLAP address?

Many workplaces provide communication technology but offer little communication support. MLAP was developed to help address that support gap.

Books and Templates

How many books are there?

The ecosystem currently contains six books covering professional communication, workplace situations, conflict, pressure, and communication support.

How many templates are included?

The ecosystem currently contains 237 communication templates, including 150 flagship templates.

Can templates be customised?

Yes. Templates are intended to provide communication support and can be adapted to suit individual situations.

Who Is It For?

Who can use MLAP?

Teachers, educational professionals, international staff, multilingual workplace employees, and professionals working across cultures may find the system useful.

Is it only for teachers?

The current system focuses primarily on educational workplaces, although many communication situations are applicable to wider professional contexts.

Can schools use the system?

Schools and educational organisations may find the communication support framework useful for staff support and professional development.
