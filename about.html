Why Message Like A Pro Exists

Message Like A Pro was developed to address a communication support gap that exists within multilingual workplaces. While communication technologies continue to improve, communication effectiveness often remains inconsistent.

Explore The Research View The Ecosystem

The Communication Support Gap

Technology can deliver messages quickly, but technology alone does not guarantee effective communication outcomes.

Communication Uncertainty

Employees often know they need to communicate but remain uncertain about what to say.

Communication Hesitation

Fear of misunderstanding, hierarchy and workplace expectations can delay communication.

Ambiguity

Expectations, responsibilities and procedures are frequently unclear in multilingual workplaces.

Lack Of Support

Organisations often provide technology but not communication support.

Research-Informed Development

Message Like A Pro was developed through the examination of communication experiences within multilingual educational workplaces. The system is informed by communication research, workplace experiences and communication challenges identified within technology-mediated environments.


The goal is not simply to improve message delivery. The goal is to improve communication effectiveness.
